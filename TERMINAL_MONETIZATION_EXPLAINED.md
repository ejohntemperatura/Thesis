**Terminal Leave and Monetization are NOT regular leave types!**

They are **leave credit conversions** - converting your accumulated leave credits into cash payment.

## ğŸ“‹ What's the Difference?

### Regular Leave (Vacation, Sick, etc.)
- **Purpose**: Take time off from work
- **Calendar**: You select specific dates you'll be absent
- **Impact**: Marked as absent in DTR/attendance
- **Late Application**: Yes, if dates are in the past
- **Example**: "I want to take vacation from Dec 10-15"

### Terminal Leave / Monetization
- **Purpose**: Convert leave credits to cash
- **Calendar**: No calendar needed - not taking time off
- **Impact**: No absence, no DTR impact - you still work
- **Late Application**: No - not date-based
- **Example**: "I want to convert 10 days of leave credits to cash"

## ğŸ’° Terminal Leave

**What it is:**
- Conversion of accumulated Vacation and Sick Leave credits to cash
- Typically used upon retirement or separation from service
- You're not taking time off - you're cashing out your credits

**When to use:**
- Retiring from service
- Separating from employment
- Want to convert accumulated credits to cash

**How it works:**
1. Select "Other (Terminal Leave / Monetization)"
2. Choose "Terminal Leave"
3. Enter number of leave credits to convert (e.g., 10 days)
4. Submit for approval
5. If approved, credits are converted to cash payment

## ğŸ’µ Monetization of Leave Credits

**What it is:**
- Conversion of accumulated leave credits to cash
- Can be done while still employed
- You're not taking time off - you're cashing out credits

**When to use:**
- Need extra cash while still employed
- Have accumulated many leave credits
- Want to monetize unused credits

**How it works:**
1. Select "Other (Terminal Leave / Monetization)"
2. Choose "Monetization of Leave Credits"
3. Enter number of leave credits to convert (e.g., 5 days)
4. Submit for approval
5. If approved, credits are converted to cash payment

## ğŸ”„ Commutation Option

Both Terminal Leave and Monetization include a **Commutation** option:

- **Not Requested**: Credits converted at regular rate
- **Requested**: Request for commutation (monetization) of the credits

This is an additional option for how the credits are processed.

## â“ Common Questions

### Q: Do I need to select dates on the calendar?
**A:** No! These are not absences. You don't need calendar dates.

### Q: Will this affect my attendance?
**A:** No. You're not taking time off, so no attendance impact.

### Q: Can I submit this for past dates?
**A:** There are no "past dates" for these - they're not date-based. You can submit anytime.

### Q: Will I be marked absent?
**A:** No. You continue working normally. This is just a credit conversion.

### Q: How many days can I convert?
**A:** Enter the number of leave credits you want to convert. This depends on your accumulated balance.

### Q: What's the difference between Terminal Leave and Monetization?
**A:** 
- **Terminal Leave**: Usually for retirement/separation
- **Monetization**: Can be done while still employed
